    • About Yonit Levy

    Yonit Levy is an Israeli news anchor, television presenter and journalist.
    Yonit was born in the French Hill neighborhood of Jerusalem, Israel.
    In October 2011, she married Ido Rosenblum, an Israeli screenwriter, director and actor. The couple has 3 children together.
    Levy became the first woman in Israel to be appointed the sole anchor position of a prime-time newscast on a commercial channel.
    In an interview to the magazine "Yediot Ahronoth", Levy said that her favorite TV shows are "The White House" and "Buffy,the Vampire Slayer".

    Q1:

    The 3 main diffrences between web1, web2 and web3 are:

    1. In web1 you can search online only with the specific URL you are looking for.
    In web2 you can serach online using a search engine (with tags).
    In web3 you can search online using a search engine (that uses a semantic search).
    2. In web1 you dont have crawlers that search for web pages, compares to web2 and web3 that uses crawlers.
    3. In web1 pages are static compares to web2 and web 3, which pages are dynamic for changes.

    Q2:

    The query "pills side effects"

    * web1: you will have to use the specific URL of a site that talks about "pills side effects".
    * web2: you can search the query. You will get sites that contains the tags "pills", "side", "effects" (not necessary together).
    * web3: you can search the query. You will get sites that connect to the topic of the query (and not necessary contains the specific words)
