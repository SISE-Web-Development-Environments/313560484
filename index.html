    • Esther Lisha

    Esther Lisha was born in 1930 in Cairo, Egypt. She got married at the age of 20 to David Lisha and they spent 58 wonderful years together.
    They immigrated to Israel in 1956. They have 6 children, 20 grandchildren and 40 great-grandchildren.
    When they arrived in Israel, they opened a falafel booth, which became a home-based restaurant serving traditional Egyptian cuisine.
    In one of the pictures below, you can see the falafel booth they owned.
    At leisure, Esther likes to watch the news and knit. She knitted a blanket for every great-grandson and grandson.
    These days, Esther lives with her caregiver Ninette. Due to the corona situation, they cannot go outside and therefore spend most of their time playing card games.
    All grandchildren and great-grandchildren make sure to talk to Esther and Ninet in video calls to spend their time pleasantly at home.

    Q1:

    The 3 main diffrences between web1, web2 and web3 are:

    1. In web1 you can search online only with the specific URL you are looking for.
    In web2 you can serach online using a search engine (with tags).
    In web3 you can search online using a search engine (that uses a semantic search).
    2. In web1 you dont have crawlers that search for web pages, compares to web2 and web3 that uses crawlers.
    3. In web1 pages are static compares to web2 and web 3, which pages are dynamic for changes.

    Q2:

    The query "pills side effects"

    * web1: you will have to use the specific URL of a site that talks about "pills side effects".
    * web2: you can search the query. You will get sites that contains the tags "pills", "side", "effects" (not necessary together).
    * web3: you can search the query. You will get sites that connect to the topic of the query (and not necessary contains the specific words)
